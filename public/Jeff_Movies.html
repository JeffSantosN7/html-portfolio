The Greatest Movies of All Time

Top 5 according to Jefferson Santos

1. 2001: A Space Odyssey (1968)

All-time sci-fi classic by the visionary director Stanley Kubrick. Challenging and complex, this movie will keep you intrigued for days.

2. The Godfather (1972)

The best movie example of a modern greek tragedy. From the music and acting to screenwriting and cinematography, this film is an exercise of perfection.

3. Schindler's List (1993)

One of the most painful episodes of human history represented with care and honesty by a master of cinema.

4. Cinema Paradiso (1988)

The greatest love letter to cinema and the art of filmmaking.

5. The Lord of the Rings: Return of the King (2003)

The ultimate fantasy epic movie. Unmatched scale, solid acting, thrilling battles and a story of true friendship and heroism.
